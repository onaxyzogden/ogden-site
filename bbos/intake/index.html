BBOS Operator Intake

This intake is the clean first step for people who want to explore fit, direction, and what it would look like to move into the BBOS system properly.

The goal is not to perform for approval. It is to get clear, fast, and honest about whether there is a real fit between what you need and what BBOS is designed to support.

What this intake is for

This is a first-pass filter. It helps surface where you are, what you are building, what kind of support or structure you may need, and whether the next conversation makes sense.
